Jiyun's Resume Game

Welcome to the game!

This game is an interactive resume, so the monster, the items that are dropped by the monsters, and the dialogue with the NPCs represent my experience.

To Move: Use WASD or arrow keys

To Attack: Press Space

To Talk to NPCs: Press E

Goal: Talk to all four characters you find on the map without being killed by the monster, and try to discover three different items that can be found when you defeat the monster.
